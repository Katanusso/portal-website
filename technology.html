Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity

Our comprehensive disaster recovery and business continuity planning ensures that we can maintain service delivery even in the face of unexpected disruptions.

Disaster Recovery

Our disaster recovery strategy ensures rapid restoration of critical systems and data in the event of disruption:

  • Redundant Infrastructure: Cloud-based systems with geographic redundancy
  • Regular Backups: Automated daily backups with secure off-site storage
  • Recovery Testing: Quarterly disaster recovery drills and testing
  • Recovery Time Objectives: Defined RTOs for all critical systems
  • Incident Response Team: Dedicated team with defined roles and responsibilities

Business Continuity

Our business continuity plan ensures uninterrupted service delivery during disruptive events:

  • Distributed Workforce: Team members across multiple locations
  • Alternative Work Arrangements: Flexible remote work capabilities
  • Communication Protocols: Clear procedures for emergency communication
  • Service Prioritization: Framework for maintaining critical services
  • Regular Testing: Annual business continuity exercises
